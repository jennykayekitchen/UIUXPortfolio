Software Development Projects
My Version

My Version is a playlist generator for Taylor Swift songs. Users can create multiple “moods” and assign one or more moods to each of her songs, and then generate a list of songs based on up to two moods that are selected.

Tech Utilized: JavaScript, REACT, JSON, HTML, with a focus on CSS and CRUD.

The ClassApp

I created the ClassApp with a classmate to help our cohort stay connected and organized. Whether we're getting together to work on a group project, looking for job leads, or we just want to hang out, the ClassApp has us covered. We can create, edit, and delete events with ease.

Tech Utilized: JavaScript, REACT, JSON, HTML, and CSS.

The Junior Tri-Wizard Tournament

The Junior Tri-Wizard Tournament is a Harry Potter themed app created to allow the students who can’t compete in the Goblet of Fire to test their knowledge of spells, potions, and famous witches and wizards. Each student can earn points for their school. Let the games begin, and may the best school win!

Tech Utilized: C#/.Net, SQL, JavaScript, React, HTML, and CSS.

About Me
In 2003, I received my senior class' "I'll Take Care of It" award, and I've been chasing that high ever since. The award showcases my lifelong desire to help others and solve problems.

I am an UI/UX designer and software developer with a passion for design and a knack for problem-solving. During my time at Nashville Software School (an intensive full-time software development bootcamp), I learned skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React, C#/.NET, and SQL, and have experience working with GitHub, VS Code, and Visual Studio.

Prior to my foray into software development, I worked in the corporate world, gaining experience in program coordination, case management, and human resources at companies such as Amazon.com and CKE Restaurants Holdings, Inc. I have a track record of providing excellent customer service and building strong relationships with both internal and external stakeholders.

I am confident that my diverse skillset and experience will allow me to create software that meets the needs of clients and users alike.
